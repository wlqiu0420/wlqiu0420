# 👋 Welcome to My Academic Profile

Wenlin Qiu (邱文林) is a Postdoctoral Fellow working in the field of **numerical analysis and partial differential equations**.

---

## 🎓 About Me

- 🎓 Position: Postdoctoral Fellow
- 📍 Research Field: Numerical Analysis, PDEs, Fractional Differential Equations
- 🧠 Interests: Nonlocal models, integro-differential equations, time-fractional diffusion
- 📊 Focus: High-order numerical schemes and error analysis

---

## 🔬 Research Interests

My research focuses on the development and analysis of numerical methods for complex evolution equations, including:

- Fractional diffusion and subdiffusion equations  
- Volterra integro-differential equations   
- Nonlocal and nonlinear PDE models  
- Finite difference, finite element, and convolution quadrature methods
